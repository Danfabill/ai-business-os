# AI Business OS - Marketing Audit Framework

## Purpose

This audit framework is used to review a small business website, tracking setup, SEO foundation, lead flow, and marketing system.

The goal is to identify what is blocking traffic, leads, sales, reporting accuracy, and future growth.

---

## 1. Business Overview

### Information to collect:

* Business name
* Website URL
* Industry
* Target customers
* Main offer or service
* Main business goal
* Current marketing concern
* Current traffic sources
* Current lead or sales process

### Key question:

What is the business trying to achieve, and what is currently blocking that result?

---

## 2. Website Structure Review

### Check:

* Homepage clarity
* Main headline
* Call-to-action visibility
* Service or offer explanation
* Trust signals
* Contact flow
* Mobile responsiveness
* Page speed impression
* Navigation clarity

### Key question:

Can a visitor quickly understand what the business offers, who it helps, and what action to take next?

---

## 3. SEO Foundation Review

### Check:

* Page titles
* Meta descriptions
* Heading structure
* Local keywords
* Service keywords
* Internal links
* Image alt text
* Indexing basics
* Search intent alignment

### Key question:

Is the website structured in a way that helps search engines and real users understand the business?

---

## 4. Tracking and Analytics Review

### Check:

* GA4 installed
* Google Tag Manager installed
* Meta Pixel installed
* Google Ads conversion tracking
* Form submission tracking
* Button click tracking
* Phone/email click tracking
* Thank-you page tracking
* Clean event naming

### Key question:

Can the business clearly measure where leads are coming from and which marketing actions are working?

---

## 5. Ads and Campaign Review

### Check:

* Campaign objective
* Landing page match
* Conversion tracking
* Audience targeting
* Ad message clarity
* CTA alignment
* Budget waste risks
* Retargeting setup

### Key question:

Are ads sending people to a page and tracking setup that can actually measure results?

---

## 6. Lead Flow Review

### Check:

* Contact form visibility
* CTA placement
* Form length
* Booking flow
* Email follow-up
* Response process
* CRM or lead tracking
* Missed lead risks

### Key question:

What happens after a visitor becomes interested, and where can leads be lost?

---

## 7. Reporting Review

### Check:

* Current reports
* Important metrics
* Vanity metrics
* Conversion metrics
* Traffic sources
* Cost per lead
* Lead quality
* Monthly decision process

### Key question:

Does the business have clear reporting that supports better marketing decisions?

---

## 8. Automation Opportunities

### Check:

* Lead follow-up automation
* Email sequences
* CRM updates
* Report generation
* Task reminders
* Client onboarding
* Review requests
* Content workflow

### Key question:

What repeated marketing or admin tasks can be simplified with automation?

---

## 9. Priority Scoring

Each issue should be scored by:

* Impact: Low / Medium / High
* Urgency: Low / Medium / High
* Difficulty: Easy / Medium / Hard
* Priority: Fix Now / Fix Later / Monitor

---

## 10. Final Audit Output

The final audit should include:

* Summary of main findings
* Top 3 problems
* Top 3 quick wins
* Tracking issues
* SEO issues
* Website conversion issues
* Lead flow issues
* Recommended next steps
* Suggested service package

---

## Audit Positioning

AI Business OS helps small businesses turn messy marketing into a clear growth system by finding the highest-priority issues first, then building better tracking, reporting, automation, and growth workflows.
