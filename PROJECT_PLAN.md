# AI Business OS - Project Plan

## Vision
AI Business OS is a future business platform for marketing, automation, CRM, client reporting, and AI-assisted operations.

## Phase 1: Service Website
Build a simple public website to offer marketing audits and monthly marketing support packages.

## Phase 2: Internal Tools
Create internal tools for lead tracking, audits, reports, proposals, and client management.

## Phase 3: Automation
Add AI-assisted workflows, reporting, follow-ups, and client onboarding.

## Phase 4: SaaS
Turn the internal tools into a paid subscription platform.

## First Milestone
Create a free Vercel-hosted website with:
- Homepage
- Services
- Packages
- Contact section
- Clear call-to-action
